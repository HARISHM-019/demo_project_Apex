Frequently Asked Questions

What is the uptime guarantee?

+

We offer a 99.9% uptime guarantee for all our hosting plans. If we fall below this guarantee, you will receive credit according to our Service Level Agreement (SLA). Our infrastructure is designed with redundancy to ensure maximum reliability.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

+

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time. The process is seamless and typically takes just a few minutes. You'll only pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Do you offer money-back guarantee?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, you'll receive a full refund, no questions asked. Domain registration fees are non-refundable.

What kind of support do you provide?

+

We provide 24/7 support via live chat, email, and phone for all customers. Our Pro and Business plan customers receive priority support with faster response times. We also have an extensive knowledge base with tutorials and guides.

Is website migration included?

+

Yes, we offer free website migration for all new customers. Our expert team will handle the entire migration process for you, ensuring zero downtime. This service is available for up to 5 websites on the Pro plan and unlimited on the Business plan.
